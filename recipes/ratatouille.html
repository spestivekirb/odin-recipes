Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a vibrant and healthy vegetarian French dish. It is the namesake of the famous 2007 Pixar movie.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini (x2)
  • Tomatoes (x2)
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper (preferably red or green)
  • Dried parsley
  • Salt
  • Grated parmesan (1 cup)

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook the garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add the eggplant, parsley and salt.
  3. Cook until the eggplant is tender, then spread the mixture in a preparred casserole dish.
  4. Sprinkle with parmesan, spread the zucchini on top and sprinkle with more parmesan.
  5. Continue to layer with the other vegetables according to the instructions in Step 4.
  6. Bake the ratatouille in the preheated oven until the vegetables are tender
  7. Serve hot!
